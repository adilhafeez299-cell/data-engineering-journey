## GUARDRAILS & ADJUSTMENTS

### If Behind Schedule
1. **1-2 weeks behind:** Add 2-3 hours to next 2 weekends
2. **3+ weeks behind:** Extend phase by 1-2 weeks, adjust timeline
3. **Focus on core:** Reduce project polish, focus on understanding

### If Ahead of Schedule
1. **Don't skip ahead:** Use extra time to deepen understanding
2. **Build additional projects:** Reinforce learning with hands-on work
3. **Review fundamentals:** Strengthen weak areas

### Certification Exam Tips
1. **Practice exams:** Aim for 70%+ before scheduling real exam
2. **Weak areas:** Drill heavily on lowest-scoring topics
3. **Hands-on:** Build projects from scratch to reinforce concepts
4. **Don't rush:** Understanding > passing quickly
5. **Retake buffer:** 2 weeks built in for retakes if needed

### Energy Management
1. **Listen to your body:** Rest when needed
2. **Weekends are key:** 70-80% of progress happens Sat/Sun
3. **Gym is non-negotiable:** Mental health foundation
4. **Adjust intensity:** Sprint weeks are intense, but not every week
5. **Celebrate wins:** Acknowledge progress along the way
